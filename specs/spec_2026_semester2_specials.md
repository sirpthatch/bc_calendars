Overview
--------
The goal is to create a calendar that we can import into google that contains information on what specials Juniper has during her school days.

Request
-------
Examine the lib/calendar_test.ics file and the lookup tables below to create a new ICS calendar that contains events for each school day that label what specials are on that day.  In particular:
1) Each week will either be an A week or a B week - that information should be available from the data in lib/calendar_test.ics
2) Events should be created for every day in the calendar_test.ics after today
3) Each event should be labeled - "J - [Name of Special]"
4) The output calendar should be located in output/juniper_spring_specials.ics

A Week Calendar
---------------
Monday - PE
Tuesday - Swim & Spanish
Wednesday - Music & Library
Thursday - Dance & STEAM
Friday - Music, Library, & Spanish

B Week Calendar
---------------
Monday - PE & STEAM
Tuesday - Swim & Spanish
Wednesday - Music & Library
Thursday - PE & Spanish
Friday - Spanish & Dance
